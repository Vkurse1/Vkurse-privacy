# Privacy Policy for ВКурсе-валют

**Effective Date:** February 19, 2026

At **ВКурсе-валют**, we value your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

## 1. Information Collection
We do **not** collect, store, or share any personal information. 
The app does not require registration, login, or any personal details.

## 2. Usage of Data
- **Exchange Rates:** To provide currency conversion, the app fetches public exchange rates from third-party APIs (ExchangeRate-API, CoinGecko). No user identifiers are sent with these requests.
- **Local Settings:** Your preferences (favorite currencies, base currency) are stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device.

## 3. Analytics
We do not use any third-party analytics services to track your usage.
